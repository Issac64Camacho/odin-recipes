Peanut Butter Sandwich

Description

My recipe for peanut butter sandwich.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • Peanut butter
  • Banana (Optional)

Steps

  1. Get 2 slices of bread
  2. Add peanut butter on one of those slices of bread
  3. Then add sliced bananas onto the bread (Optional)
  4. Then close it with the other slice of bread
  5. Enjoy!
