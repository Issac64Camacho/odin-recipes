Ramen

Description

This is how I eat my instant ramen.

Ingredients

  • One pack of instant ramen (Any flavor)
  • One or two eggs
  • Water
  • Paprika
  • Parsley flakes
  • Black pepper
  • Leftovers (Optional)

Steps

  1. Boil water in a pot
  2. Add paprika, parsley flakes, and black pepper
  3. Add ramen to boiling water
  4. Then add eggs immediately
  5. Wait for 1 to 2 mins
  6. Pour contents into a bowl
  7. Add seasoning
  8. Enjoy!
