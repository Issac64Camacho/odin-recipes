Apple Smoothie

Description

This is my recipe for an apple smoothie that I drinked a few days ago.

Ingredients

  • One Apple
  • A few tablespoons of peanut butter (I like alot in mine)
  • Milk or Almond Milk
  • Oatmeal
  • Cinnamon
  • Cocoa Powder
  • Berries (Optional)

Steps

  1. Slice apple into small pieces
  2. Add pieces into blender
  3. Add peanut butter into blender
  4. Add oatmeal into blender
  5. Add cinnamon and cocoa powder
  6. Add berries into blender (Optional)
  7. Then add milk or almond milk.
  8. Blend until smoothie texture
  9. Enjoy!
